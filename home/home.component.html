About Us

Welcome to SD Hospital, where compassionate care meets cutting-edge medicine. Located in Salem, Tamilnadu, we've been dedicated to our community's health for 5 years, earning a reputation for excellence. Our expert team of physicians, nurses, and support staff provides round-the-clock emergency care, a full spectrum of medical and surgical services, and specialized care in cardiology, oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and more. Our advanced diagnostic imaging and rehabilitation services support accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans. For women's health needs, we offer comprehensive obstetric, gynecologic, and mammography services. Access your medical records, schedule appointments, and find educational resources conveniently through our patient portal. We accept various insurance plans and offer billing assistance to ease financial concerns. Contact us today to experience healthcare excellence with a compassionate touch at SD Hospital.
